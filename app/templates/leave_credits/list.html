Leave Credits

Defaults to the current calendar month. To accrue the previous calendar month instead, choose “Previous calendar month (clear year)” in the month list and clear the year field. VL/SL rows use the last day of that month. January accrual also posts Jan 1 grants of 3 SPL and 5 WL (for anyone appointed before that Jan 1; Jan 1 hires get those only via the appointment ledger row). December accrual posts Dec 31 rows to zero out unused SPL/WL. New SPL/WL on appointment are recorded when an eligible employee is saved with an appointment date. Undo accrual removes those automated rows for the chosen period for every employee (same year/month rules as Run accrual), writes deletion snapshots for audit, and recomputes balances. It does not delete manually entered ledger lines unless they exactly match the system accrual wording and dates.
